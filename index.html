Risa Tobin

Risa is an undergraduate student completing a Bachelor's of Music from McGill Univeristy. She has been playing trumpet for 11 years and has performed with groups such as the All-Eastern Orchestra and McGill Wind Orchestra. Alongside her passion for performance, Risa is deeply interested in how music affects cognitive processes. Through her work with the Yale Alzheimer's Disease Research Center, and Belief Lab she has been able to explore these topics.
